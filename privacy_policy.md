# Privacy Policy

**Alfred Family Assistant**

*Last updated: 2025*

---

Alfred Family Assistant ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you opt in to receive SMS communications from us.

---

## Information We Collect

When you opt in to receive SMS messages from Alfred Family Assistant, we collect your mobile phone number and your SMS communication preferences.

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use your mobile phone number solely to send you information related to your Alfred Family Assistant service, including family calendar reminders, event notifications, appointment confirmations, and scheduling updates.

**We do not share, sell, rent, lease, or transfer your mobile phone number, SMS opt-in consent, or opt-in data to third parties, affiliates, or lead generators for marketing or promotional purposes.**

---

## Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your calendar activity and preferences.

Message and data rates may apply.

## Opt-Out

Reply **STOP** to any message to opt out. Reply **HELP** for help.

## Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.
